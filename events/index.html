Wine Tastings

Every week, experience a different a different region of wine.

Every Tuesday at 6pm $10/person

Join us on Tuesday evenings on an adventure of the palate to exotic and beautiful regions through our vast selections of wine. Our wine tastings are a very fun and affordable way for the inexperienced to gain some knowledge about wines, as well as wine connoisseurs to taste wines from all over.

Our wine tastings are hosted by Mr. Troy Dorrance, who has a great passion for exquisite wines and wineries. If you'd like more information, please don't hesitate to contact us.

As always, feel free to stop by during our business hours. We look forward to hearing from you!
